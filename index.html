Mission and Vision
In line with our mission and vision, we aim to eliminate the gender gap in engineering and STEAM around the world and raise awareness on the subject.
The proportion of women in STEAM is lower than the proportion of men, and this is the case in many countries around the world. Some statistics show that:

1. In the USA, 27% of those working in STEAM fields are women.
2. In the European Union, only 17% of those working in STEAM are women.
3. In Turkey, 22.3% of scientists are women, compared to 10.7% of engineers.
4.In OECD countries, only 43% of PhD holders in STEAM fields are women.
5. Worldwide, only 35% of students studying in STEM fields at universities are women.

These data show that women are underrepresented in STEAM. This shows that policies and programs should be developed to encourage girls to take an interest in STEM fields and pursue careers in these fields.
