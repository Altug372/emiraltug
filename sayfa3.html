Limor Fried

Limor Fried is a US electrical engineer and founder of Adafruit Industries. Limor Fried is a recognized pioneer in the Open source hardware community. Limor Fried develops various educational materials and products to encourage young people in the field of engineering. Adafruit is a platform that offers educational materials and products in the STEM field, so it is very useful for young people who are interested in STEM.
