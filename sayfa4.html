Melinda Gates

Melinda Gates is the co-founder of the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, the world's largest private foundation. The foundation works in the areas of global health, education, poverty alleviation and development. Melinda Gates is the president and chair of the board of directors of the foundation. Melinda Gates plays an important role in the education of women and girls, access to healthcare and gender equality. She has initiated various projects and initiatives around the world on these issues.
