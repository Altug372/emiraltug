Reasons why female students fail in STEAM

Why are schools failing girls in tech careers? There are many reasons why girls are failing in tech careers. Here are some of these reasons:

1.Gender discrimination: As the majority of those working in tech are male, some female students may experience gender discrimination. This can reduce girls' confidence in technology, which can negatively affect their success.

2.Lack of role models: Successful women in technology can be role models for young girls. However, the lack of these role models can discourage girls from pursuing technology careers.

3.Access to educational opportunities: In some schools, girls may have fewer opportunities for technology education than boys. This can lead to girls' underachievement in technology careers.

4.Prejudices: Some teachers may have preconceptions that girls will fail in subjects such as math and science. These prejudices can cause girls to fail in technology careers.

5.Lack of emotional and social support: Girls may lack emotional and social support if they do not have a family member or friend who works in technology. Lack of these supports can lead to the failure of female students in technology careers.
